Generic Attack:
A broad category of attacks that exploit common vulnerabilities.
Often automated and high frequency.

Exploits:
Attacks that use known software vulnerabilities to gain access.

DoS:
Denial of Service attacks aim to make systems unavailable.

Reconnaissance:
Scanning and probing activity to gather system information.

Backdoor:
Hidden methods to bypass authentication and maintain access.

Shellcode:
Malicious payloads designed to execute arbitrary code.
